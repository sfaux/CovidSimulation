Welcome to VirusBreak

This game allows you to see how viral outbreaks spread through a population. You can watch the spread of well known viruses such as measles or ebola, or make up your own.

As well as looking at the spread of your infection you can also play with counter-measures such as quarantine and vaccination to slow or even stop the spread of the disease. You can see how these measures can be effective in stopping even the most infectious diseases, and how losing these controls can allow disease to re-emerge.

Playing the game

Playing the game is a simple process:

  • From the Viruses section pick one of the standard viruses to load its properties.
  • From the Properties section you can see how virulent the infection you selected is.

    You can change the properties of the virus if you like by dragging the sliders. The values you set here will affect the spread of the virus in the simulation.
  • Start the simulation. On mobile press the Run tab, or on desktop look at the main area.

    The coloured squares represent the whole of the UK population (around 66 million people). The colour of the square represents the status of the people. At the start everyone will be either vaccinated (and therefore immune), or uninfected (but vulnerable). You will also see one purple square which is the start of an initial infection.

    Press the Run button. The simulation will start to run.

    You will see your initial infection start to spread. Sometimes it will naturally die out - usually because of your vaccination or quarantine countermeasures. Sometimes the whole of the popualtion will end up infected. Watch the numbers in the categories at the top to see the effect your outbreak had. You can start further infections at any point by pressing the Infect button.
  • The simulation will stop when the last infection dies out. You can look at the final tally of infections and deaths at the top of the simulation area. Try changing the properties of your virus to see what effect it has on the outcome. Press the Reset button to clear the results and start again.
